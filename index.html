Venue

Our celebration will take place at the beautiful Hotel Vincci Selección La Plantación del Sur in sunny Costa Adeje, Tenerife.

Ceremony

The ceremony will be on the hotel's lawn overlooking the sea and white sand Duque Beach. The weather in Costa Adeje during April is typically between 20-23 degrees but could get chilly once the sun goes down.

  • April Forecast

Reception

The reception to follow will be held close by in the La Jaima Restaurant of the Hotel. Provided over 35 guests join us to celebrate we will be having a BBQ Buffet. Later into the evening, for drinks and dancing, we will move inside to the Mirador Jazz Club.

Flights

The hotel is a 14 minute taxi ride from Tenerife South Airport (TFS). There are flights every Tuesday and Friday from Bournemouth Airport or you can fly from other UK Airports anytime.
