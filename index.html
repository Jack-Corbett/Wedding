Activities

We have been visiting Tenerife for many years so wanted to share some of our favourite places to visit and things to do while you're on the island.

  • See all on a Map

Siam Park

Siam Park is rated as the best water park in the world on Tripadvisor and it's always a great day out.

We are planning a visit on Sunday the 16th of April (2 days after the wedding) if you'd like to join us, the more the merrier!

Karting Las Americas

Karting Las Americas is a Go Kart track a 20 minute walk from the Wedding Hotel.

We went for the first time last year and had great fun. It would also be good with a big group if people are interested!

Teide National Park

Mount Teide is the volcano at the centre of Tenerife which is the tallest peak in Spain.

You can take a cable car up to the top for an incredible view and there are many walking trails which take you through pine forests and over rocky volcanic terrain.

Duque Beach

Our favourite white sand beach in Tenerife which is right in front of the Wedding Hotel.

Barranco del Infierno

A hiking trail through the mountains to a waterfall with stunning views, a 12 minute taxi ride away.

Puerto Colón

There is a huge range of water sports available. Parascending, Jet Skis and Whale Watching boat trips.

The Duke Shops

A complex with a few shops, nice restaurants and bars just down the hill from the Wedding Hotel.

Siam Mall

Siam Mall is a lovely shopping centre right next to Siam Park with a great range of shops and places to eat.

Plaza del Duque and The Corner

Two lovely shopping complexes a short walk from the Duque beach.

Restaurants

We have also included some of our favourite restaurants below that we always come back to.

  • See all on a Map

Rosso Sul Mare

A beautiful Italian restaurant with excellent service on the seafront in Calleta, a quaint finishing village, perfect for a romantic evening.

La Tosca

Although technically also Italian, La Tosca has a wide range of great food options for a reasonable price close to the Wedding Hotel.

Prime 400

Excellent and reasonably priced steaks as well as lots of other options with great cocktails, the Kiwi Daiquiri is lovely!

Harley's

A good range focusing on American food and if you like you can dine in a classic car, although we'd recommend sitting inside.

Hard Rock Cafe

A bit further away in Las Americas but it is well worth the visit for the food and there's a really good crazy golf course opposite.

Yum Yum Thai

Next to Prime 400 in the Duke's shopping centre, it offers a great range of Thai cuisine and cocktails.

Cafe la Bahía

Our favourite ice cream place at the back of the Duque beach, they have an incredible range of flavours.

Beach Club Bahía del Duque

A great spot at the back of the Duque beach for a drink or snack. The Sangria is good.

Coqueluche Beach Bar

Casual beach bar with good drinks. A good place to sit and watch the sea or the paragliders landing on the beach.

Flights

The hotel is a 15 minute taxi ride from Tenerife South Airport (TFS). For guests travelling from Southampton, both TUI and Ryanair fly from Bournemouth on Tuesdays as well as Fridays and Saturdays respectively. However, if you are able to travel up, flights from larger airports like Gatwick and Heathrow offer more flexibily and are much more reasonably priced (less than half the cost of Bournemouth flights).

Accommodation

Hotel Vincci Selección La Plantación del Sur is offering us special rates for wedding guests so you won't have far to go. But there are lots of other great places to stay in the area and we've included some of our favourites below. If you're a larger group there are also lots of apartments/villas available nearby through Airbnb.

Wedding Venue



  • View

Our Wedding Hotel is 15 minutes by taxi from the Airport.

If you're intersted in staying here, contact us directly for the price. The hotel offers a range of rooms from villas with private terraces and whirlpool tubs to sea view double rooms and suites.

Meliá Jardines del Teide



  • View

The Meliá Hotel is 15 minutes by taxi from the Airport and a 7 minute walk to the Wedding Venue.

Although we haven't stayed here before, it's really close by, well reviewed and is slightly more affordably priced than our Wedding Venue for similar quality.

H10 Costa Adeje Palace



  • View

The H10 is 15 minutes by taxi from the Airport and a 20 minute walk to the Wedding Venue.

This family friendly hotel is right on the front, reasonably priced and offers a range of catering options. The walk to the venue is quite up-hill so you might prefer to get a taxi.

GF Isabel



  • View

The GF Isabel is 14 minutes by taxi from the Airport and a 13 minute walk to the Wedding Venue.

If you're looking for something with extra space and a kitchen, the GF Isabel is a complex of apartments and bungalows with terraces or gardens which still offers a range of catering options.

Los Olivos



  • View

Los Olivos Beach Resort is 14 minutes by taxi from the Airport and a 17 minute walk to the Wedding Venue.

We have stayed here many times and Jack's Grandparents owned an apartment here for 35 years. There are 1 and 2 bedroom apartments with terraces or gardens in a family friendly complex.

Yucca Park



  • View

Yucca Park is also 14 minutes by taxi from the Airport and a 17 minute walk to the Wedding Venue.

We have stayed here many years ago, the apart-hotel is in a great location and is very reasonably priced if you don't mind self catering (there are lots of great restaurants and food shops nearby).
