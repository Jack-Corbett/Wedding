Venue


Our celebration will take place at the beautiful Hotel Vincci Selección La Plantación del Sur in sunny Costa Adeje, Tenerife.

Ceremony

The ceremony will take place on the hotel's lawn overlooking the sea and white sand Duque Beach. The weather in Costa Adeje during April is typically between 20-23 degrees but could get chilly once the sun goes down, so bring an extra layer for the evening. Dress code formal.

Reception

The reception to follow will be held close by in the La Jaima Restaurant of the Hotel. We will be having a BBQ Buffet with something for everyone to enjoy. Later into the evening, for drinks and dancing, we will move inside to the Mirador Jazz Club.

  • View the Menu

Flights

The hotel is a 15 minute taxi ride from Tenerife South Airport (TFS). For guests travelling from Southampton, both TUI and Ryanair fly from Bournemouth on Tuesdays as well as Fridays and Saturdays respectively. However, if you are able to travel up, flights from larger airports like Gatwick and Heathrow offer more flexibily and are much more reasonably priced.

Accommodation

Hotel Vincci Selección La Plantación del Sur is offering us special rates for wedding guests so you won't have far to go. But there are lots of other great places to stay in the area and we've included some of our favourites below. If you're a larger group there are also lots of apartments/villas available nearby through Airbnb.

Wedding Venue



  • View

Our Wedding Hotel is 15 minutes by taxi from the Airport.

If you're intersted in staying here, contact us directly for the price. The hotel offers a range of rooms from villas with private terraces and whirlpool tubs to sea view double rooms and suites.

Meliá Jardines del Teide



  • View

The Meliá Hotel is 15 minutes by taxi from the Airport and a 7 minute walk to the Wedding Venue.

Although we haven't stayed here before, it's really close by, well reviewed and is slightly more affordably priced than our Wedding Venue for similar quality.

H10 Costa Adeje Palace



  • View

The H10 is 15 minutes by taxi from the Airport and a 20 minute walk to the Wedding Venue.

This family friendly hotel is right on the front, reasonably priced and offers a range of catering options. The walk to the venue is quite up-hill so you might prefer to get a taxi.

GF Isabel



  • View

The GF Isabel is 14 minutes by taxi from the Airport and a 13 minute walk to the Wedding Venue.

If you're looking for something with extra space and a kitchen, the GF Isabel is a complex of apartments and bungalows with terraces or gardens which still offers a range of catering options.

Los Olivos



  • View

Los Olivos Beach Resort is 14 minutes by taxi from the Airport and a 17 minute walk to the Wedding Venue.

We have stayed here many times and Jack's Grandparents owned an apartment here for 35 years. There are 1 and 2 bedroom apartments with terraces or gardens in a family friendly complex.

Yucca Park



  • View

Yucca Park is also 14 minutes by taxi from the Airport and a 17 minute walk to the Wedding Venue.

We have stayed here many years ago, the apart-hotel is in a great location and is very reasonably priced if you don't mind self catering (there are lots of great resturaunts and food shops nearby).
