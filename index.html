Venue

Our celebration will take place at the beautiful Hotel Vincci Selección La Plantación del Sur in sunny Costa Adeje, Tenerife.

  • See on a Map

Ceremony

The ceremony will be on the hotel's lawn overlooking the sea and white sand Duque Beach. The weather in Costa Adeje during April is typically between 20-25 degrees but could get chilly once the sun goes down.

  • Learn More

Reception

The reception to follow will be held close by in the La Jaima Restaurant of the Hotel. Later into the evening for drinks and dancing we will move inside to the Mirador Jazz Club.

  • Learn More

Accommodation

Hotel Vincci Selection La Plantacion (link) is offering us special rates for wedding guests so you won't have far to go. But there are lots of other great places to stay in the area and we've included some of our favourites below.
